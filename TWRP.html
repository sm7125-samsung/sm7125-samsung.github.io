TEAMWIN ANDROID RECOVERY (TWRP) INSTALL

  1. Follow all the preinstall steps from here.
  2. Download TWRP for your device. (A52) (A72)
  3. Turn off your phone, connect it to PC and reboot to download mode by holding down Volume up, Volume down and power button at the same time.
  4. Open Odin and check if your device is detected, then untick "Autoreboot" in its settings.
  5. Put the tar file you downloaded into the AP tab
  6. Click start and wait for the installation to finish, Odin will show PASS
  7. Now hold down Power button and Volume down for about 10 seconds to force reboot the device
  8. Immediately after the screen goes black release the keys and hold down Volume up and Power button at the same time until you enter TWRP (You may need to release the keys after about 20-30secs due to the "unlocked bootloader" warning)
  9. If you properly followed all the steps you should now see the TWRP menu.
  10. I HIGHLY recommend to flash multidisabler and right after you enter TWRP to disable forced encryption and stock recovery restoration.
