PIXEL EXPERIENCE INSTALL

  • PE Recovery
  • TWRP/OFox
  1. Follow all the preinstall steps from here.
  2. Download the PixelExperience zip and recovery tar from here: A52 | A72. Simply download the latest files.
  3. Download the patched VBMeta tar for your device from here: Download
  4. Power off the device and connect it to a PC.
  5. Now hold Volume Up + Volume Down + Power buttons until you enter Download Mode.
  6. Now, confirm by pressing Volume Up once.
  7. Open Odin and make sure your phone is detected (it says "Added!" in the Message window).
  8. Put the recovery .tar file you downloaded into the AP tab.
  9. Put the vbmeta .tar file you downloaded into the USERDATA tab.
  10. Once the phone reboots after pressing "Start", hold Volume Up + Power buttons until you reach PixelExperience recovery.
  11. Click "Start", the phone will automatically reboot. Now hold Volume Up + Power buttons for at least 10 seconds until you boot into PixelExperience recovery.
  12. If you properly followed all the steps you should now see the PixelExperience recovery menu. If not, repeat all steps.
  13. Now click Factory Reset and confirm the process.
  14. Now select "Apply Update" and then "Apply from ADB" on the phone to sideload the PixelExperience .zip package. Alternatively, copy the PixelExperience zip to your phone's SD card and select "Choose from SD card".
  15. On your Computer, sideload the PixelExperience package using adb sideload PixelExperience.zip
  16. Once you have installed everything successfully, click the back arrow in the top left corner of the screen, then “Reboot system now”.
  17. If you did everything correctly then your phone will reboot into PixelExperience.
  1. Follow all the preinstall steps from here.
  2. Install OrangeFox using the guide here, or TWRP using the guide here.
  3. Download the PixelExperience build for your device A52 | A72. Google Apps are included.
  4. Copy the PixelExperience zip to your phone's SD card or a USB Storage, or use adb sideload method.
  5. Flash the PixelExperience zip using the "Install" button menu.
  6. After that go to "Wipe", "Format data" and confirm with "yes", otherwise you may end up in a bootloop if using encryption or coming from stock ROM.
  7. Again go into the "Wipe" menu, select "Advanced Wipe" and tick metadata and cache partitions.
  8. If you did everything correctly then your phone will reboot into PixelExperience.
