PREINSTALL INSTRUCTIONS

Unlock Bootloader

  1. Connect the device to a Wi-Fi network.
  2. Go to Settings, "About phone", "Software Information" and press the "Build Number" option until it asks you to unlock Developer Mode.
  3. Go back into Settings, there should be a new option called "Developer Options" at the bottom now.
  4. In the Developer Options search for "OEM Unlock" and enable it.
  5. Turn off your phone and connect it to a PC.
  6. Boot your phone into download mode: Hold Volume Up + Volume Down + Power button
  7. Once you are in Download Mode, hold the Volume Up button to enter "Device unlock mode".
  8. Then press the <Volume Up button once to unlock the bootloader.
  9. Your device will reboot twice and erase your data, you can now unplug the USB cable from your device.
  10. Run through Android Setup and skip everything you can, then connect the device to a Wi-Fi network.
  11. Re-enable Developer Options by clicking the “Build Number” option 7 times, in the “Settings” app within the “About Phone” menu, and verify that “OEM Unlock” in Developer Options is enabled and greyed out.
  12. You have now successfully unlocked your bootloader.
  13. You can now install TWRP or OrangeFox if wanted.
